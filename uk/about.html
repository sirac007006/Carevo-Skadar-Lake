About Carevo Skadar Lake

Welcome to Lake Skadar – Experience the Magic of Nature with Us

Our Story

A family tradition of sharing Lake Skadar's beauty with visitors from around the world

Our Family Heritage

For over 15 years, our family has been passionately running a business dedicated to lake tourism. As hosts from a lakeside village on the shores of Lake Skadar, we have lived alongside this magical lake for generations – we know every corner, every story, and every hidden beauty that we want to share with you.

Our deep connection to this land gives us unique insights into its history, ecology, and culture that can only come from a lifetime of experience. What began as a small family operation has grown into the region's premier tour provider, while still maintaining our commitment to authentic, personalized experiences.

Our Unique Experiences

We organize boat cruises and kayaking adventures, perfect for anyone wanting to discover the peaceful, authentic, and untouched side of Lake Skadar. Sailing on calm waters, you'll discover ancient island monasteries, hidden coves, rich birdlife, and breathtaking landscapes.

Our experienced hosts will gladly share interesting stories and legends about the lake, as well as recommend the best spots for photography and relaxation. If you love adventure, kayaking is an ideal opportunity to explore narrow channels and quiet lagoons at your own pace, immersed in nature, away from crowds and noise.

The Carevo Difference

What sets Carevo Skadar Lake apart is our deep local knowledge and genuine passion for this remarkable ecosystem. Our guides aren't just tour operators – they're locals who have grown up on and around the lake, with generations of knowledge about its hidden gems, wildlife, and history.

We believe in small group experiences that allow for personal attention and flexibility. Whether you're interested in birdwatching, photography, fishing, history, or simply relaxing in nature, we tailor each tour to ensure it meets your interests and exceeds your expectations.
