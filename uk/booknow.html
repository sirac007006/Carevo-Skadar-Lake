Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tours and booking process

What should I bring on the tour?

We recommend bringing sunscreen, a hat, sunglasses, a light jacket (even in summer as it can get cool on the water), comfortable clothes, and a camera. For fishing tours, all equipment is provided. Drinking water is provided on all tours.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance during the regular season and 1-2 weeks in advance during the peak summer months (July-August) to ensure availability. For large groups, earlier booking is advised.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel free of charge up to 24 hours before your tour. Cancellations less than 24 hours before the start time may be subject to a cancellation fee. In case of bad weather conditions, we will offer you an alternative date or a full refund.

Are the tours suitable for children?

Yes, most of our tours are family-friendly and suitable for children of all ages. We provide life jackets for all passengers, including appropriate sizes for children. The Sunset Tour and Birdwatching Tour are particularly enjoyed by families.

Do you offer private tours?

Yes, we offer private tours for individuals, families, and groups who prefer a more personalized experience. Private tours can be customized to your interests and schedule. Please contact us directly for pricing and availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash (EUR), credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and PayPal. For tour bookings, we require a 20% deposit to secure your reservation, with the remaining balance due on the day of the tour.

Have Questions?

If you need any additional information or have specific requirements, don't hesitate to contact us directly.
