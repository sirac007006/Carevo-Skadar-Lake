Our Story

Our Beginnings

Carevo Skadar Lake was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to share the breathtaking beauty and rich heritage of Lake Skadar with visitors from around the world. What began as a small family operation has grown into the region's premier tour provider, while still maintaining our commitment to authentic, personalized experiences.

Our journey started over a decade ago when our founder, a local who grew up on the shores of Lake Skadar, decided to share his deep knowledge and passion for this unique ecosystem with travelers seeking to explore Montenegro's natural treasures.

Our Mission

At Carevo Skadar Lake, our mission is to provide visitors with an authentic, immersive experience that goes beyond typical tourism. We strive to create meaningful connections between our guests and the natural environment, local culture, and traditions of the Lake Skadar region.

We are committed to sustainable tourism practices that help preserve this precious ecosystem for future generations while supporting local communities. Every tour we conduct is designed with respect for nature and local traditions at its core.

The Carevo Difference

What sets Carevo Skadar Lake apart is our deep local knowledge and genuine passion for this remarkable ecosystem. Our guides aren't just tour operators – they're locals who have grown up on and around the lake, with generations of knowledge about its hidden gems, wildlife, and history.

We believe in small group experiences that allow for personal attention and flexibility. Whether you're interested in birdwatching, photography, fishing, history, or simply relaxing in nature, we tailor each tour to ensure it meets your interests and exceeds your expectations.
